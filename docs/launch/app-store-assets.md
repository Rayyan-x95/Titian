## App Description

Short:

Titan is your all-in-one productivity system to manage tasks, notes, and expenses in one place.

Long:

Titan is an installable, offline-first productivity app that combines Tasks, Notes, and Finance into one streamlined system. Capture work, ideas, and spending in one place, sync your flow across modules, and stay productive even without internet.
